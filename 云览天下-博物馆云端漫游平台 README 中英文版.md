## 📌 项目简介 \| Introduction

### 中文简介

《云览天下》是一款基于**鸿蒙HarmonyOS 6\.0**原生开发的综合性博物馆云端漫游平台，聚焦全球文博文化数字化传播。项目整合全球主流博物馆资源、珍贵馆藏藏品数据、历史科普互动工具，打破线下观展的时空限制，为用户打造沉浸式云端文博学习、漫游、科普体验。

本项目于2025年完成开发与应用商城上架，凭借创新性的文博数字化方案、优质的鸿蒙原生交互体验，成功斩获**2025鸿蒙开发者计划万元激励金**。现全程开源源代码，供鸿蒙开发者学习、二次开发、技术交流与开源共建。

### English Introduction

**Cloud Museum** is a comprehensive cultural and museum roaming platform natively developed based on **HarmonyOS 6\.0**\. It focuses on the digital dissemination of global museum culture, breaking the time and space limitations of offline exhibitions\.

The project integrates massive global museum resources, precious collection data, and interactive historical science popularization tools, providing users with an immersive online cultural experience\. Completed and launched on the HarmonyOS App Market in 2025, this project won the **2025 HarmonyOS Developer Program 10,000 RMB Incentive Award** for its innovative digital cultural solution and high\-quality native interaction experience\.

The full source code is now open\-sourced for HarmonyOS developers to learn, secondary develop, communicate and co\-build the HarmonyOS ecosystem\.

## ✨ 核心功能 \| Core Features

### 中文功能介绍

- **🌐 全球博物馆馆藏浏览**：收录世界绝大多数主流博物馆资料，涵盖场馆介绍、馆藏分区、特色展品、场馆背景文化等全方位信息，足不出户漫游全球博物馆。

- **🏺 珍贵藏品详情查阅**：海量文物藏品高清展示，附带藏品年代、工艺、历史背景、文化价值、馆藏出处等专业科普信息。

- **❓ 历史知识竞赛问答**：内置海量文博、历史趣味题库，支持互动答题模式，兼顾趣味性与知识性，轻松积累历史文博知识。

- **📅 历史时间轴溯源**：可视化中外历史发展时间轴，清晰梳理朝代更迭、重大历史事件、文物诞生背景，直观构建历史知识体系。

- **⚖️ 古代度量衡转换工具**：实用便民工具，支持古代长度、重量、容量、货币等度量衡与现代单位一键换算，适配历史学习、文史研究需求。

- **📱 鸿蒙原生极致体验**：适配鸿蒙6\.0及以上版本，遵循鸿蒙原生设计规范，流畅度高、兼容性强、交互简洁轻量化。

### English Features

- **Global Museum Browse**: Collect data of most mainstream museums around the world, including venue introduction, collection zones, classic exhibits and cultural backgrounds\.

- **Precious Collection Display**: High\-definition display of massive cultural relics with professional popular science information such as age, craftsmanship, historical background and cultural value\.

- **Historical Knowledge Quiz**: Built\-in massive interesting question banks of cultural relics and history, supporting interactive Q\&A mode for knowledge accumulation\.

- **Historical Timeline**: Visual Sino\-foreign historical timeline, sorting out dynasty changes and major historical events to build a systematic historical knowledge framework\.

- **Ancient Metrology Converter**: Practical tool for one\-click conversion between ancient and modern units of length, weight, capacity and currency, suitable for historical learning and research\.

- **Pure Native HarmonyOS Experience**: Adapted to HarmonyOS 6\.0 and above, following native design specifications with high fluency and compatibility\.
